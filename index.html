Earth

Welcome to Earth, the only planet we know of that hosts life! Covered in vast oceans, sprawling forests, and bustling cities, this blue gem is home to over 8 million species (and counting). Fun fact: Earth isn’t a perfect sphere—it’s slightly squished at the poles, making it an oblate spheroid. Fancy!

Moon

Welcome to the Moon, Earth’s steadfast companion! With its cratered surface and silvery glow, the Moon has inspired countless myths, songs, and even scientific breakthroughs. Fun fact: The Moon is gradually drifting away from Earth—about 3.8 cm per year—but it still plays a crucial role in creating ocean tides and stabilizing Earth’s wobble. Amazing!
